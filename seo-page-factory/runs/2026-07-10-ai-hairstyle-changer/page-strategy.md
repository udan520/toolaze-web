# AI Hairstyle Changer Page Strategy

## 核心定位

上传一张清晰人像，通过可视发型卡片和可编辑 prompt，预览 Bob、Pixie、Bangs、Long Layers、Braids、Buzz Cut、Fade 等发型方向。

页面服务“先看效果再决定”的视觉探索，不承诺专业剪发或真实结果。

## 交互策略

- Pattern: `preset-first-with-custom-prompt`
- Upload: 1
- Default preset: Layered Bob
- Visual cards: 11
- Free prompt: always visible
- Hidden model branding: yes
- Hero demo: 16:9 before/after
- Model selector: hidden

## 页面结构

1. Hero + Tool
2. What Is
3. How To
4. Popular Hairstyle Ideas
5. Photo Tips
6. AI Preview vs Real Haircut
7. Use Cases
8. Features
9. Reviews
10. FAQ
11. Related AI Tools

## SEO 语言

主标题：

`Free AI Hairstyle Changer: Try Hairstyles on Your Photo`

副文案必须包含：

- one clear portrait
- hairstyle presets
- editable prompt
- no signup
- no watermark

# AI Video Generator Qualified Free Copy Design

## Goal

Restore the commercially useful `Free AI Video Generator` wording without implying unlimited use or access without registration.

## Verified Basis

- New accounts receive 10 credits once after sign-up.
- Grok 1.5 Video starts at an estimated 3 credits for eligible settings.
- Therefore, 10 sign-up credits are enough for at least one eligible Grok video generation.

## Copy Changes

- Meta title: `Free AI Video Generator Online | Text & Image to Video`.
- H1: `Free AI Video Generator Online`.
- Meta description and hero description must state that the 10 free credits are received after sign-up and apply to at least one eligible Grok generation.
- Add a direct FAQ explaining the same conditions.
- Do not use `unlimited free`, `free forever`, or `no sign-up` for video generation.

## Reusable Rule

Toolaze pages may use `Free` when a verified quota or sign-up grant can complete a real generation. Visible copy must disclose the access condition, credit amount when relevant, and any model or setting limitation. It must not imply unlimited usage or no registration unless those claims are separately verified.

## Verification

- Contract test requires `Free` in the metadata title and H1.
- Contract test requires the 10-credit sign-up condition and eligible Grok scope in visible copy.
- Meta description remains at or below 160 characters.
- Existing model-dropdown Quality ratings remain unchanged.
